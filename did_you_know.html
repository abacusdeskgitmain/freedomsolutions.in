Did You Know ?

LIFE: Live-In-Full-Ecstasy

It is a privilege to live a human life! Our Soul can have ecstatic experiences through our body & mind.

Such ecstasy can only be felt if we live our life with fullness by adhering to natural laws of the Universe.

If one seeks ecstasy through un-natural measures like drugs, drinking, smoking etc then, he is not living LIFE but living a LIE as the “F” for Fullness shall be missing.

One resorts to such un-natural ways of ecstasy only to temporarily fill the emptiness caused by negative happenings in one’s life, which in turn causes a bigger void to be filled leading to a vicious circle.

Live LIFE so abundantly that GOD becomes proud of you!

STRESS: Some-Things-Require-Easy-Solutions

The word “STRESS” is used quite frequently in today’s modern world as if it is an important & integral part of us. Literally, it means “a state of exerted pressure!”

Let us now interpret the above meaning with a metaphysical viewpoint! Any obstruction in our way can only be removed by putting an extra effort or pressure. To enable the same, we need extra energy. Common practice of today’s man is to penalize himself by borrowing that extra energy from his existing quota of energy which ultimately leads to a situation called “Over-Exertion.” Regular Energization of the body & mind can provide that much needed extra energy!

Stressful situations actually demand a clear & receptive state of mind for easy & efficient solutions. To maintain that state of mind, regular practice of pranayama & meditation is now mandatory.

So next time whenever you feel “stress,” remind yourself that Some Things Require extra energy & clarity of mind for Easy Solutions. From that standpoint, rather than making stress as your integral part, it’s better to choose Energization of body & mind, pranayama & meditation to be a regular part of our schedule.

PAIN: Pay-Attention-In-Need

Pain is nothing but a symptom to remind that there is an unresolved emotional issue lying underneath and now needs your attention.

If you are taking pain-killers and suppressing the symptom every time it comes, then beware, it might manifest in a bigger form!

We can learn the art of talking to the pain and explore which emotion is requiring our attention to get resolved in order to live a smooth & happy life.

ILL: It-Lacks-Love

Whenever we are ILL, remember its love which is in deficit in our system!

Great Healers around the world have recognized that there is a Clown Chakra just above our Heart or Thymus point which gets blocked.

So, remember whenever you feel sad or low, just tap at the centre of your chest and loudly say “I TOTALLY & COMPLETELY LOVE & ACCEPT MYSELF.”
