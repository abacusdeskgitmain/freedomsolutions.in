Chakra Clearing & Balancing

Freedom offers a very simple but powerful technique to clear and balance your main energy distribution centers popularly known as Chakras. There are seven main Chakras and are also called as the Seven Gates to Freedom! If the life force energy flows smoothly through all the chakras, then one doesn’t invite unnecessary problems in life.

Chakras are energy vortices along meridians or energy lines of the human body. There are 7 main chakras and many minor ones.

The physical body of the human is coated or surrounded by his energy or etheric body. The chakras are thus an interface between the human and cosmic energy. They function like energy gateways through which energy flows in and out of the physical system because of their spinning motion. On the energy body, the 7 major chakras are located on the central energy meridian known as ‘Sushumna Nadi’.

A human, essentially, keeps drawing energy for survival and growth from two prime sources:
Spiritual Universal energy from the cosmos.
Earth energy from Mother Earth.

Both these energies are continuously absorbed by a human through the chakra network, and in turn are supplied to the system. Excess energy is flushed out in the same manner through this network.

The location of the chakras along the energy meridian is aligned with major endocrinal glands of the human body. As we already know the relation between ‘Thought-Emotion-Energy-Physical Body’ we can therefore conclude that any thought which generates an emotion, in turn affects the corresponding chakra, which in turn influences the endocrine glands. The endocrine glands secrete hormones which in turn has an impact on the functioning of the system.

When a human is in harmony, his thought process and emotional response system is balanced. This in turn enables the chakras to spin at the appropriate vibration thus ensuring a smooth flow of energy to the physical state. The individual is thus HEALTHY.

On the other hand, when an individual is disturbed by any thought process, the disturbance generates an inappropriate emotional response, which in turn causes the corresponding chakra to malfunction. Over a period of time, the continuous chakra malfunction impedes the energy flow to the corresponding gland in the physical body. This manifests as DIS-EASE in the form of symptoms at the physical and mental levels.

Using the concept of Reversibility, we understand that if there is a diseased state then by clearing the energy block of the affected chakra we can heal the emotional disturbance. This in turn facilitates clarity at the level of thought and helps an individual restore himself to a healthy, harmonious state of existence.
